# Section 537. Inducement to be appointed as liquidator, etc.

Any person who gives or agrees or offers to give to any member or creditor of a company any valuable consideration with a view of securing his own appointment or nomination, or to securing or preventing the appointment or nomination of some person other than himself, as the companyâ€™s liquidator, commits an offence and shall, on conviction, be liable to imprisonment for a term not exceeding three years or to a fine not exceeding one hundred thousand ringgit or to both.
