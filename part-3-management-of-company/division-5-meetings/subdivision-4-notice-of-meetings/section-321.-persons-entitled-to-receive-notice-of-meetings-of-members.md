# Section 321. Persons entitled to receive notice of meetings of members

\(1\) Notice of a meeting of members shall be given to every member, director and auditor of the company.

\(2\) The reference to a member in _subsection \(1\)_ includes any person who is entitled to a share in consequence of the death or bankruptcy of a member who, but for his death or bankruptcy, would be entitled to receive notice of the meeting and the company has been notified of the personâ€™s entitlement in writing.
